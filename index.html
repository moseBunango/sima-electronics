Welcome to Sima Electronics, your one-stop destination for high-quality electric appliances. At Sima Electronics, we specialize in providing a wide range of products including extension cables, main switches, circuit breakers, sockets, and various types of bulbs to meet your electrical needs.

Located in the bustling Ferry Area of Kigamboni, Dares Salaam, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch products coupled with exceptional customer service. Whether you're a homeowner, electrician, or business owner, we have the solutions you need to power up your projects.

At Sima Electronics, customer satisfaction is our priority. We welcome everybody, from individuals looking for a single bulb to contractors in need of bulk orders. Our team is dedicated to providing personalized assistance and expert advice to ensure you find the perfect products for your requirements.

Can't make it to our store? No problem! We offer nationwide delivery, so you can enjoy the convenience of shopping from the comfort of your home or office. Wherever you are in Tanzania, we'll ensure your order reaches you safely and swiftly.
